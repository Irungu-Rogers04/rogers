Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to register my vehicle?

To register your vehicle, you typically need your vehicle information, your personal informatio like identification number.

What is the registration process for new vehicles?

The process for registering a new vehicle is that you need to register an account if you dont have one and you can log in if you have one.
After logging in you can access the registration tab under services and fill in the fields required.

What is the registration process for used vehicles?

The process for registering a used vehicle is similar to that of a new vehicle, but you may also need to provide additional documentation such as a bill of sale or transfer of ownership form.

How long will it take for my registration to be approved?

On submitting the registration form they will be check on and verified and the registration will be approved. It takes at-least two working days for your registrationto be approved.

How often do I need to renew my vehicle registration?

You typically need to renew your vehicle registration annually.

How can i report my lost vehicle?

To report about a lost vehicle or a stolen one you need to log in and access the service under the services tab. And the involed authorities will be notified.
